Stanley

A story for kids about a bird rescue
Buy

The book “Stanley, the Sparrow” is based on a true story. Stanley was a bird that my family rescued when I was about 13 years old. He lived with us for somewhere between 12 and 15 years, no one in the family can remember for sure. He was one of many animals we rescued over the years, but Stanley had the most personality.
